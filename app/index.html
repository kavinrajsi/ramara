We saw a problem, we made it ours...

Fresh, farm-picked almonds are often passed between dozens of hands at harvest, and travel thousands of miles across various intermediaries.

They see multiple shipping vehicles, retail warehouses, re-packaging shop floors and delivery fleets before they reach the retail stores.

This is bound to affect the quality and price.

We wished for Ramara to be the solution. We made it happen!

HERE'S HOW!

We bring best quality almonds at the best price, straight from our Californian farms, with a strategically improvised supply chain management, that cuts down on all intermediaries.

We are committed to provide the best-quality almonds with delightful taste and the best possible price!

And, we would like to do business with you.
